We do not collect sensitive personal data including biometric information, digital images, or photographs of users.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal data, including:

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We uphold strict confidentiality regarding your personal information:

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements
