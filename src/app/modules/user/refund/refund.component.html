Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 01/01/2025

Returns Policy

Our return policy lasts 5 days from the date of purchase. After this period, we cannot offer a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. You must provide proof of purchase. We only process refunds for transactions that meet our return criteria.

Refund Process

Approval Process

After receiving and inspecting your return request, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your request. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Approved Refunds

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund yet, first:

  • Check your bank account again (processing times may vary)
  • Contact your credit card company or bank - it may take some time before your refund is officially posted
  • If you've done all of this and you still have not received your refund, please contact us at aeown.in

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription or service at any time by contacting us through our Contact page.

Important Notice:

Cancellation requests made less than 10 business days before the next billing cycle will take effect in the following billing period. You will retain access to the service until the end of your current billing period.
